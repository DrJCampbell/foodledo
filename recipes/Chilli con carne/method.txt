Pre-heat the oven to 150 Celcius and get the rice going in the rice cooker. In an oven proof pan, add the choppen onions and garlic and fry. Add the fresh chilli and chilli powder and cumin plus seasoning.

Add the mince/chuck steak and brown. Add the tinned tomatoes and paste plus 125 mL of water (for four people). Stir and add the cinnamon stick.

Bring to the boil. Add a paper cover over the surface. Cook for 1.5 hours in the oven. Add the kidney beans and cook for anothe 30 mins.
