Heat the cocnut oil in a large pan. Chop the chorizo and fry in the oil for a minute. Add the slices onion and soften. Increase the heat and add the chopped chicken breast along with salt and pepper. Stir fry for three minutes until the chicken is cooked. Add the cherry tomoatoes (sliced in half) and cook for a minute. Add the spinach and stir through until wilted. Turn off the heat. Break the mozarella into pockets in the mixture and allow to melt. Spoon onto a plate and top with pine nuts. Serve with crusty bread.
