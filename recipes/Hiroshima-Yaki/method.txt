cook the noodles and cool in water
mix a batter with flour and water
heat a large flat metal pan on the hob
spread a circle of batter and leave to set
top with bonito, cabbage and spring onion
cover with slices of bacon
flip over and allow the bacon to cook
make a pile of noodles next to the stack and mix in some sauce
place the stack on the noodles
crack an egg next to the stack and spread out
place the stack on the egg and flip
serve with a sprinkle of aonori and japanese mayo
