Cut bacon to 1 cm-wide strips and fry until almost cripsy.

Boil pasta in slated water for ~10 mins

Mix egg yolks, cream, parmesan plus salt and pepper in a jug

Crips the bacon. Drain the pasta from the water (not too dry). Return to the pan (off the heat) and add the creamy mixture. Stir to coat the pasta.

Serve on a plate, with cripsy bacon and extra parmesan.
