Fry the sausages gently. Midway through cooking, cover the pan with a lid to retain moisture. Boil salted water and cook the linguini for 10 mins. Chop the sausages into small pieces and set aside. Soften the shallots, then add the tomoatoes. Deglaze the pan with white white and lemon juice. Add the sausage pieces and warm through. Let the liquid reduce by half and then mix in the creme fraich. Add salt and pepper to taste. Drain the linguini and add to the pan. Serve topped with grated parmesan.
