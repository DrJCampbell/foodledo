From Nigella Bites, p14.
Preheat oven to 220 Celcius. Poach the salmon in water with lime leaves for 20 mins. Soft boil the eggs for 10 mins then cool under running water and peel once cooled. Remove the fish from the liquor and reserve. In a frying pan, heat the oil and butter, then add the chopped onion and soften. Add the spices and fry for a minute. Then add the rice and stir to coat in the onion and spices. Add the rice and the the liquor to the rice cooker and set to quick. After cooking, flake in the fish, lime juice and zest, a splash of fish sauce and the chopped corriander leaves. Stir and plate up. Top with an egg and more chopped corriander.
