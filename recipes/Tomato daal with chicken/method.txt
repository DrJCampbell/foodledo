cover the split peas in warm water and leave to soak for 20 mins. Melt the oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and bay leaf and fry for 30 seconds. Add the onion and stir fry for 3 minutes. Add the garlic, chilli and ginger and stir fry for a minute. Add the turmeric and garam masala and stir for 30 seconds. Add the tomatoes and chicken stock and bring to the boil. Drain and rinse the split peas and add to the pan. Simmer for 40 mins, stirring regularly and adding more water as required. When the daal is nearly ready, heat oil in a frying pan and cook the chicken with some salt and pepper. Stir the corriander through the daal and serve, topped with chicken.
